Interview

1. What first sparked your interest in software development, and when did you realize it was something you wanted to pursue?

"I got into software development after a coding class in high school. I enjoyed building things and realized I wanted to keep learning more about it."

2. Were there any specific experiences or people that inspired you to choose this path?

"Yes, my cousin is a software engineer and really encouraged me to try coding. Seeing how much they enjoyed it made me curious and excited to try it myself."

3. What are some skills or areas within software development that you’re most excited to learn and work on?

"I’m really interested in web development and artificial intelligence. I think it would be fun to learn how to make websites and work with AI."

4. How do you see a degree in software development helping you reach your career goals? Do you have a particular career path or type of company in mind?

"Getting a degree will give me the skills and help me find a good job in the field. I’d like to work for a company that uses tech to help people, like in healthcare or education."

5. What challenges or changes are you expecting in the software development field, and how do you plan to keep up with them?

"Software changes fast, so keeping up can be tough. I plan to keep learning by taking online classes and joining coding groups."
